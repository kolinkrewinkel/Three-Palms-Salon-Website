Three Palms Salon & Day Spa offers a full-service experience in downtown Livermore, California. From basic cuts to massages, every variety of treatment and service imaginable is available at Three Palms.

Our highly-skilled and professionally-trained hair stylists can maintain your existing fabulous look, guide you to a new look, or transform you into a sample image you provide! Professional-quality beauty supplies are used, ensuring the health of your hair. For your convenience, the same products used to treat you in the chair are also available for sale at great prices within the salon.

For a full spa day experience, we also provide massages, facials, energy healing, and pilates. Each room offers a unique style and atmosphere to ensure a relaxing and therapeutic environment.

Prices are set by individual stylists and may differ based on hair length or thickness. Customization requests to a service may change the price; contact your stylist for a quote.

For friends, gift cards for usage on any service are available.
